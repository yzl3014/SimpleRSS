# SimpleRSS

![preview](./assets/preview.png)

SimpleRSS is a RSS reader that runs on Xiaomi MiBand 9 Pro. It downloads files in specific formats from the internet, processes them, and makes them available for you to read. Whether it’s blogs, social media, forums, or news sites — as long as they have a supported RSS feed — you can use this app to read them. It supports features such as one-tap saving to your local storage, automatic saving of RSS URLs, and quick URL entry.

Note: Support for the Redmi Watch 5 and its eSIM version is not yet fully optimized.

Watch the demo video on Bilibili: https://b23.tv/T2xJCDv
